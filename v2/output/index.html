Hello! I'm Nicholas Vincent. I study how data flows make AI work – from the contributions we make to the systems that use them. Below you will find a brief bio and links to my work.

Recent news: | Wrote several newsletters and did an interview with Business Insider on DeepSeek's R1, data distillation, and the live-by-the-sword, die-by-the-sword data paradigm. | Moderated a panel and gave a talk at the NeurIPS 2024 Workshop on Creativity & Generative AI. | New preprint on a framework for attentional agency. | Attended ICWSM 2024 (helped organize the Data Challenge). | Attended CHI 2024 (where students presented work on OpenWebUI, auditing GenAI, data consent issues, and tools for governance). | Received a SIGCHI Outstanding Dissertation Award (coverage). | Spoke at the Digital Democracies Institute. | Panelist at University of Toronto Fairness - ChatGPT workshop put on by the Data Sciences Institute.
