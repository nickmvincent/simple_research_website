Hello! I'm Nick. Below, you can find a short bio describing my body of work.


Quick bio: Prof. Nick Vincent is a researcher in human-centered artificial intelligence (AI). His research focuses on understanding how many forms of data -- including works and content that we produce and logs of our behavior -- provide value to AI systems such as search engines, recommender systems, and new "generative AI" systems. A key theme from his findings is that we all contribute to the success of AI systems through data contributions, which means by organizing in groups we might exert "data leverage" to bargain for larger shares of the profits of AI or for more voice in how AI systems are governed. The overarching goal of this research is to work towards an ecosystem of highly capable and widely beneficial AI technologies that mitigate inequalities in wealth and power rather than exacerbating them.
